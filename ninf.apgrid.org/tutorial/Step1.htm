1.        Login and Test Globus

1.1.      Prerequisite

ü         Your Globus certificate is placed in your home directory on TITECH PRESTO-III.

Ø         ~/.globus/usercert.pem

ü         Pass phrase of your encrypted private key is the same with your login password on PRESTO-III.

ü         Your Globus certificate is NOT on AIST F32.

Ø         Run Ninf-G client on PREST-III.

E     In order to run Ninf-G client, you need to copy your Globus certificate and key to F32.

ü         A list of PRESTO-III hosts is on the web
  http://ninf.apgrid.org/ISSGC/

 

1.2.      Login to Presto IIII

ü        login to nimbus.titech.hpcc.jp using ssh

Ø        You can not login to PRESTO III directly from your desktop
% ssh  eugs00@nimbus.titech.hpcc.jp
 

E    replace with your account

E    account & password is on the paper sheets

ü        login to pad???.titech.hpcc.jp from nimbus using rsh
      % rsh  pad002.titech.hpcc.jp

E    replace with any host on the list

 

1.3.      Test Globus

ü        % env | grep  GLOBUS

ü        % grid-proxy-init

Ø        Enter your login passphrase

ü        % globusrun  –a  –r  pad???.titech.hpcc.jp

ü        % globus-job-run  pad???.titech.hpcc.jp  /bin/hostname

ü        % globusrun  –a  –r  fsvc003.asc.hpcc.jp

ü        % globus-job-run  fsvc003.asc.hpcc.jp  /bin/hostname
