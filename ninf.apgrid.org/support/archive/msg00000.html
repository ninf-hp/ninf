Dear friends,

The ApGrid web/mail server in AIST has been replaced with new machine.
Taking this opportunity, I'd like to propose the restructure of ApGrid
mailing lists as below:

core@apgrid.org (We have already used this ML for the discussion)
  Used for administrative discussions.
  Authorized members are subscribed to this ML.
  
tech-contacts@apgrid.org (new ML)
  Used for technical discussions.
  Authorized members are subscribed to this ML.

discuss@apgrid.org (new ML)
  Used for open discussions.
  Anyone can subscribe to this ML on the web page.

For every ML, a list of subscribed members and archives can be browsed
on the ApGrid home page.   The web page for the subscription to
discuss@apgrid.org is also provided.
Please visit ApGrid home page (http://www.apgrid.org/) and follow the
"Email Lists" link on the left side menu.

In order to accelerate the testbed development, we should have closer
and deeper technical discussion through email and
tech-conatcts@apgrid.org is expected to be used for the discussion.

Last February, I asked you to provided the information of your
organization/projects and contributing resources.  After that, I got
some answers from you and current tech_contacts@apgrid.org includes
some members who are indicated as "Technical Contacts" in your
reports.  If you are interested in joining the discussion but not yet
included in tech_contacts@apgrid.org, please let me know and I am
willing to included you to this ML.

The current resource status of the testbed is also available on the
ApGrid home page.  Please follow the "Testbed" link on the left side
menu.

Comments, suggestions and questions are appreciated.
