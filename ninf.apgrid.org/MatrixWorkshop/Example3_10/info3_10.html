Matrix Information 

	Let A = a[i][j] be the n*n matrix defined by

		a[i][j] = i / j 	if i = j

	For example, when n = 4,
                     -             -
                     |  1  1/2 1/3 1/4| 
		A =  | 1/2  1  2/3 1/2| 
                     | 1/3 2/3  1  3/4|
                     | 1/4 1/2 3/4  1 |  
	 	     -                -
Structure: Dense, Symmetric
