Matrix Information 

	For arbitrary constants a1,a2,...,an-1,
	 let A[n] = A[n](a1,a2,...,an-1) = a[i][j]
	 denote the n*n matrix defined by

		a[i][j] = 1,	j >=i,
	        a[i][j] = aj,   j < i.

	For example,

		       -             -	
		       | 1  1  1  1  |
		A[4] = | a1 1  1  1  | 
		       | a1 a2 1  1  | 
  		       | a1 a2 a3 1  |
		       -    	     - .
Structure: Dense, Unsymmetric
