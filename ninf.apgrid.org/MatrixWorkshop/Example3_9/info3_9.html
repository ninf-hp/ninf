Matrix Information 
	Let A = a[i][j] be the n*n matrix given by

		a[1][j] = 1,            j = 1,2,...,n,

		a[i][j] = 1 / (i+j-1),  i = 2,3,...,n.

	For example, when n = 4,
                    -                 -
                    |  1   1   1   1  |
		A = | 1/2 1/3 1/4 1/5 |
                    | 1/3 1/4 1/5 1/6 |
                    | 1/4 1/5 1/6 1/7 |
		    -                 -
Structure: Dense, Unsymmetric
